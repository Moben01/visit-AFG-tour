Afghan Shopping Tips

  • 1. Bargaining is expected in bazaars - start at 50% of asking price
  • 2. Verify authenticity of lapis lazuli and carpets
  • 3. Carry small bills for easier transactions
  • 4. Check export regulations for antiques
  • 5. Visit early morning for best selection
  • 6. Ask for certificates for precious stones

Shopping in Afghanistan: FAQs

Top souvenirs include handwoven carpets, lapis lazuli jewelry, Afghan pashminas, traditional copperware, and saffron from Herat.

Most traditional markets operate on cash only (Afghanis or USD). Larger stores in Kabul may accept credit cards.

Reputable carpet and jewelry shops often arrange international shipping. For larger items, DHL and FedEx operate in major cities.
