Services Offered

Larmond helps travelers and businesses explore Afghanistan with comfort, safety, and tailored experiences. Here are answers to the most common questions.

We provide a wide variety of tours, including:

  • Cultural and historical tours across Afghanistan
  • Adventure experiences such as hiking and exploring nature
  • Business and MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, Exhibitions) packages
  • Customized itineraries based on your interests

Yes, absolutely! At Larmond, we specialize in creating tailored packages. Whether you want to focus on culture, nature, or business events, we adapt the itinerary to suit your preferences, group size, and budget.

Your safety is our top priority. We partner with trusted local guides, follow government travel advisories, and ensure secure transport and accommodations. Our team is available 24/7 for assistance during your trip.

We recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks in advance for regular tours. For large MICE events, booking 2–3 months ahead ensures the best arrangements and availability of venues.

Cancellations made 14 days before the start date receive a full refund. For cancellations made within 7 days, a partial refund (50%) applies. Unfortunately, last-minute cancellations (less than 48 hours) are non-refundable due to prior bookings.
