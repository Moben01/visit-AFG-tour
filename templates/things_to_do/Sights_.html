Must-See Sights & Attractions in Afghanistan

  • Exploring the ancient ruins of the Buddhas of Bamiyan, a UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Visiting the historic Blue Mosque in Mazar-i-Sharif with its stunning turquoise tiles
  • Walking through the vibrant bazaars of Kabul and Herat for traditional crafts
  • Discovering the Minaret of Jam, another UNESCO site hidden in the mountains
  • Admiring the architectural beauty of the Friday Mosque in Herat
  • Touring the National Museum of Afghanistan in Kabul
  • Seeing the ancient citadels like the Arg in Herat and Bala Hissar in Kabul
  • Exploring the stunning Band-e-Amir lakes, Afghanistan's first national park
